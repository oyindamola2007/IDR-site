About IDR

An industry-led institute for digital, cyber, and technology risk.

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-built training and deployment institute dedicated to preparing practitioners for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in highly regulated and high-consequence environments.

Working in collaboration with leading UK universities, IDR blends academic insight with hands-on industry practice. Learners work on real projects, with frameworks and tooling used by financial services, critical infrastructure, and technology organisations.

Industry-designed pathways Embedded deployment into risk teams Regulatory-aligned content Mentors from live environments

Industry-led training

Cohorts are designed with practitioners, ensuring capabilities match the realities of digital risk, cyber defence, and technology operations.

Academic collaboration

Programmes integrate UK university research, bringing the latest thinking on emerging risk, AI, and resilience into applied learning.

Deployment-first model

Graduates are supported through deployment into organisations, closing the gap between education, workforce readiness, and long-term capability building.
